### Rules
#### Basic Rules
* Each player has 4 pieces starting at home
* Roll a 6 to bring a piece out of home
* Move pieces clockwise around the board
* Get all 4 pieces to the center to win
#### Special Rules
* Rolling a 6 gives you another turn
* Landing on an opponent's piece sends it home
* Safe squares (colored) protect your pieces
* Must roll exact number to reach center

In our web game, you can only play when you are signed in. Please log in using your google account (standard)

### Troubleshooting
Sometimes when entering the web game, it can take up to 50 seconds to load the game because we are using render free plan to host the server and the deployment sleeps after 15 minutes of inactivity.
